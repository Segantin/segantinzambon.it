We print photos at the seat, in Lido di Venezia, with the cutting-edge technology Fujifilm Frontier and using Fujicolor Chrystal Archive type Gold Photo Paper.

The Fujifilm Frontier laboratory together with the Photographic Paper type Gold guarantee the best quality that the photo printing can: bright colors and beautiful pictures forever.

This not only affects the magnification of wedding photo but all the photographs printed by us, including the specimens.

For the wide format we have an Epson printer (paper width 61cm, length as you want) with the original Epson color with carbon pigment subordinate to the Fujifilm Print Hunter Software to get high quality enlargements on pearl paper from 290gr. and even canvas prints.
